Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Type Perfectly handles information when you use typeperfectly.com.

Information stored on your device

Type Perfectly may use browser local storage to remember information such as your personal best, recent typing sessions, selected settings, and related performance data. Guest progress generally remains on your device until you clear your browser data.

Account information

When you create an account, we may receive and store information such as your email address, account identifier, and typing progress. This information is used to authenticate your account, save your results, and provide account features.

Google sign-in information

If you choose to sign in with Google, Google may provide basic account information such as your name, email address, profile picture, and a unique account identifier. Type Perfectly uses this information only to create or authenticate your account and provide account-related features.

Type Perfectly does not receive or store your Google password.

Information you provide

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address, name, message, and any other information you choose to include. We use this information to respond to your inquiry, provide support, and protect the website.

Typing and performance information

Type Perfectly may process information such as typing speed, accuracy, scores, selected practice mode, session duration, typed character count, and weak-key performance. Signed-in users may have this information saved to their accounts.

Analytics and technical information

Type Perfectly and its service providers may automatically process limited technical and analytics information, including IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, timestamps, requested pages, referral information, security logs, and general website usage information.

We may use services such as Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the website and to improve its performance, features, and user experience.

How information may be used

  • To operate, maintain, and improve Type Perfectly.
  • To create and authenticate user accounts.
  • To save typing progress and account preferences.
  • To provide typing results and practice recommendations.
  • To respond to support, feedback, or contact requests.
  • To detect abuse, errors, fraud, or security threats.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

Service providers

Type Perfectly may use third-party service providers for authentication, database storage, analytics, hosting, security, and email services. These providers may process limited information only as needed to provide their services.

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help host, secure, analyze, or operate the website. Information may also be disclosed when required by applicable law or when reasonably necessary to protect users, rights, safety, or the website.

Data retention and control

Guest typing data may remain in your browser until it is cleared, removed through browser settings, or replaced by newer data. Account and typing information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to provide account features, maintain security, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

You may request access to or deletion of your account information by contacting us at contact@typeperfectly.com .

Children's privacy

Type Perfectly is a general typing-practice service. We do not knowingly request sensitive personal information from children. A parent or guardian who believes a child has provided personal information may contact us.

Third-party links and services

The website may contain links to or use services provided by third parties, including Google. Their handling of information is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the website, its features, technology, or legal requirements change. The updated date at the top of this page will show the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email contact@typeperfectly.com .
