Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Type Perfectly handles information when you use Type Perfectly.online.

Information stored on your device

Type Perfectly may use browser local storage to save information such as your personal best, recent typing sessions, selected settings, and related performance data. This information is generally stored on your device and is not an account.

Information you provide

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address, name, message, and any information you choose to include. We use that information to respond to your inquiry, provide support, and protect the website.

Technical information

Our hosting, security, or email providers may automatically process limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, timestamps, requested pages, and security logs. This may be necessary to deliver, secure, and maintain the website.

How information may be used

  • To operate and improve Type Perfectly.
  • To remember local typing preferences and progress.
  • To respond to support or contact requests.
  • To detect abuse, errors, or security threats.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help host, secure, or operate the website, or disclosed when required by applicable law or necessary to protect rights and safety.

Data retention and control

Local typing data remains in your browser until it is cleared by you, removed by browser settings, or replaced by newer data. Email messages may be retained for support, security, or record-keeping purposes for as long as reasonably necessary.

Children's privacy

Type Perfectly is a general typing practice service. We do not knowingly request sensitive personal information from children. A parent or guardian who believes a child has provided personal information may contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the website, technology, or legal requirements change. The updated date at the top of this page will show the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, email contact@Type Perfectly.online.
