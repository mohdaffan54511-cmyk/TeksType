About TypeTeks

About Us

Last updated: July 12, 2026

TypeTeks is a focused typing practice website built to help people improve speed, accuracy, rhythm, and confidence through short, practical sessions.

What you can practice

  • Common words, bigrams, trigrams, code, and business vocabulary.
  • Quotes, Hinglish passages, and useful English conversations.
  • Timed sessions with live WPM and accuracy estimates.
  • Personal bests, recent sessions, and keys that may need more practice.

Our goal

Our goal is to make typing practice simple, useful, and enjoyable. TypeTeks is designed for students, professionals, freelancers, programmers, and anyone who wants to type with greater control.

How TypeTeks works

You choose a content mode and time limit, then begin typing. The website compares your input with the displayed text and shows live performance information. Your best score and recent sessions may be stored locally in your browser so that you can track progress on the same device.

Keep improving:

Small, regular sessions are usually more useful than rare, very long sessions. Focus on accuracy first, then build speed.
