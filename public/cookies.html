Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how TypeTeks uses cookies and similar browser technologies.

Our current use

TypeTeks does not intentionally use advertising cookies or analytics cookies in its current basic version. The website may use browser local storage to remember typing progress and settings.

Local storage

Local storage is similar to a small browser memory area. TypeTeks may store your personal best, recent sessions, selected options, or related practice information on your device. This helps the website remember progress without requiring an account.

Essential technical storage

Our hosting, security, font, or other service providers may use necessary technical cookies, logs, or similar technologies to deliver content, prevent abuse, maintain security, or keep the website working.

How to control storage

You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Doing so may remove saved TypeTeks progress and preferences. Blocking essential browser storage may affect how some features work.

Future changes

If TypeTeks later adds analytics, advertising, accounts, or other services that use additional cookies, this policy may be updated and a consent tool may be added where required.

Contact

Questions about cookies or browser storage may be sent to contact@typeteks.online.
