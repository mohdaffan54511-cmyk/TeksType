Your privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Type Perfectly handles information when you use the website.

Guest use and local storage

You may use Type Perfectly without creating an account. Guest typing preferences, personal bests, and recent sessions may be stored locally in your browser until you clear your browser data or the data is replaced.

Optional accounts

If you choose to create an account, we process your email address, authentication information, account identifier, and the typing results you choose to save. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and are not stored directly by Type Perfectly.

Saved typing sessions

Signed-in users may save information such as mode, duration, WPM, accuracy, score, character count, and session date. This allows progress to be available across devices.

Service providers

Type Perfectly may use Supabase for authentication and database services and Cloudflare for hosting, delivery, security, and technical logs. These providers may process information needed to operate their services.

Technical information

Hosting and security providers may automatically process limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, timestamps, requested pages, and security logs.

How information may be used

  • To operate, secure, and improve Type Perfectly.
  • To authenticate optional user accounts.
  • To save and display typing progress.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To detect abuse, errors, or security threats.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

Sharing and selling

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate the website or disclosed when required by applicable law or necessary to protect rights and safety.

Data retention and control

Guest data remains in your browser until cleared. Account and saved session information may remain while your account is active or as reasonably needed for security, support, and legal obligations. Use the Contact page to request account or data deletion.

Children's privacy

Type Perfectly is a general typing practice service. We do not knowingly request sensitive personal information from children. A parent or guardian who believes a child has provided personal information may contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the website, technology, or legal requirements change. The updated date at the top of this page will show the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, use the Contact page.
